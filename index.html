About teens innovation week

In a rapidly evolving world, the Teens Innovation Summit empowers and prepares the next generation of leaders to drive positive change. This groundbreaking event, specifically tailored for teenagers between the ages of 12 and 19, serves as a catalyst for innovation, collaboration, and problem-solving. Bringing together high school students, educators, and experts from various industries, The Summit creates a supportive and collaborative environment for self-directed exploration, innovation and communication using STEAM pedagogies, and edTech tools.

Objectives of the teens innovation week

Harnessing the Untapped Potential

Teenagers possess a wealth of untapped potential, brimming with fresh perspectives, boundless creativity, and a natural inclination to challenge the status quo. They are more likely to take risks and more willing to change their minds when confronted with new information. The Summit will harness this potential by providing a platform for young minds to share their ideas, dreams, and aspirations. By nurturing their innovative thinking and problem-solving skills, we enable teenagers to become active contributors to their communities and agents of change.

Encouraging Entrepreneurial Spirit

Entrepreneurship is a driving force behind economic growth and development. By hosting the Summit, we will encourage this spirit among teenagers and provide them with a new lens through which to regard the world. Through mentorship, workshops, and interactive sessions, participants are empowered to transform their ideas into viable business ventures. This not only cultivates a culture of innovation, problem solving and creative thinking, but also paves the way for a prosperous future for both individuals and the nation.

Addressing African Challenges

The Summit is specifically designed to challenge teenagers to act on African challenges. By aligning with the AU Agenda 2063 (The Africa We Want) we direct their focus towards issues that directly impact the continent's growth and development. With a special emphasis on SDGs 4 and 13 (Quality Education and Climate Action respectively), we provide a platform for teenagers to address these pressing concerns through innovative solutions and collaborative efforts.

Empowering Global Citizens

In an increasingly interconnected world, it is crucial for teenagers to develop a global perspective and become responsible global citizens. The Summit offers a unique opportunity for young minds to engage in cross-cultural exchange, forge international connections, and broaden their horizons. Through interactive sessions, cultural showcases, and networking opportunities, participants gain a deeper understanding of global issues, fostering empathy, tolerance, and a sense of shared responsibility.

Nurturing Leaders of Tomorrow

The Teens Innovation Summit serves as a breeding ground for future leaders. By providing teenagers with opportunities to develop leadership skills, collaborate with like-minded individuals, and learn from experienced mentors, we nurture their potential to become influential change-makers. This summit equips participants with the confidence, resilience, and knowledge necessary to navigate complex challenges and shape the world they want to live in.
